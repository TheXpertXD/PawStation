About Us

PawStation is an online, searchable database of animals who need homes.

Our Mission

To use Internet technology and the resources it can generate to:

- Increase public awareness of the availability of high-quality adoptable pets
- Increase the overall effectiveness of pet adoption programs across North America to the extent that the euthanasia of adoptable pets is eliminated
- Elevate the status of pets to that of family member

From the comfort of their personal computers, pet lovers can search for a pet that best matches their needs. They can then reference a shelter’s web page and discover what services it offers. PawStation also includes discussion forums, a pet-care resource directory and a library of free pet-care articles to help keep pets in their homes.

PawStation is updated DAILY.

Organizations wishing to participate should register. A contact person should be responsible for direct communications with PawStation. Only nonprofit organizations will be included (but Federal 501(c)(3) status is not necessary).

PawStation is made up of animal-care professionals and regular people volunteering for their local animal welfare organizations all working together to maintain active and accurate homeless pet lists. Most animal welfare volunteers have “real jobs” by day. The success and the magnitude of this project is largely due to their tireless efforts to make a difference. If you would like to help, let us know! You can find us at https://www.pawstation.com/. Email: pets@pawstation.com.

If you have a Website and would like to help us promote shelter pets as the #1 choice for a new pet, you may use our free widgets and graphics to add PawStation to your website. You may also want to check out our Featured Pet Module which allows you to show PawStation pets from your community on your own home page.
