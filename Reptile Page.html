Our Reptiles!

Reptiles can be great for first-time pet owners! They are relatively low maintenance and are unique enough to be conversation starters with all sorts of people! Just don't forget about a heat lamp!

Look at the table below for the most up-to-date list on the reptiles that are up for adoption:

Lets take a closer look at who wants to get adopted!

Red


Red is one of the first reptiles we stumbled across in the alleys of Boston. Despite being a green iguana, she features a unique red design all across her scales.
Red requires a fairly large acquarium so make sure it's ready and setup before acquiring her.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Dragon


Despite his small size and spotted appearance, Dragon features the brave and ferocious traits of the fantasy creature he gets his name from! Dragon is very active and enjoys being handled by humans, a unique facet for leopard geckos!
